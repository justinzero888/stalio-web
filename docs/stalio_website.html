How stalio works

Built for doing, not planning

Most habit apps ask you to set goals, pick schedules, and design your perfect routine before you've done a single thing. Stalio starts on the other end.

Zero-setup start

Open Stalio for the first time and your day is already waiting — 6 universal habits pre-loaded and ready to check off. No onboarding questionnaire. No "what are your goals?" No empty screen.

A growing habit library

48 habits across 6 categories — health, mindset, productivity, finance, social, and home. Each researched, described, and ready to add to your day in one tap. The community keeps adding more.

Resilience over streaks

Miss a day? Stalio doesn't reset your progress to zero. It shows you a consistency score — how often you showed up over time. One hard week shouldn't erase 60 days of momentum.

Identity, not tasks

Every habit in Stalio is framed around who you're becoming, not what you're doing. "I am someone who moves their body" is a different motivation than "exercise 30 min." The language matters.

Community-powered library

The habit library grows with every user. Submit a habit that works for you, vote on others, share stories, ask questions. The best habits get surfaced to everyone.

Insight over data

Stalio doesn't just track — it tells you what it notices. "Days you meditate, your consistency score is 40% higher." Real patterns, plain language. No dashboards to decipher.
